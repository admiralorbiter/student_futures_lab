# Student Pathway Advisory Team — Core Inquiry Packet

*Hickman Mills pathway inquiry studio*

Use evidence from the app and class discussions to make a real recommendation for PREP-KC.

---

## Main question

**Which pathways look strongest for Hickman Mills students when you consider opportunity, reachability, and support needs?**

Your two products: (1) a small-group recommendation for PREP-KC and (2) an individual "My Pathway Reality Check."

There is not one perfect answer. Your job is to make a clear, evidence-based case and name what would make a pathway feel real for students.

## Pathways you will compare

1. Healthcare
2. Business / Finance / Operations
3. Manufacturing / Construction / Skilled Trades
4. Logistics / Supply Chain
5. Tech / Data
6. Teaching / Education
7. Law / Public Service

## 5-class roadmap

1. What matters to us?
2. What looks strong on paper?
3. What changes for Hickman Mills?
4. Where could someone realistically start?
5. What should PREP-KC do next?

Use this packet across all five class periods. Bring it to each discussion, and keep your notes tied to the evidence you actually saw.
