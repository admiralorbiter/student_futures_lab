# Daily Evidence Catcher

*Use one section per class period.*

The goal is not to write everything down. The goal is to capture the strongest evidence, what changed in your thinking, and what questions still matter.

## How to use this

- Bring this to each class period.
- Use it to record the strongest evidence you saw, not just random details.
- Be honest about what changed in your thinking.
- At the end of each class, star one note you think PREP-KC most needs to hear.
